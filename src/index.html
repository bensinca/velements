Vision

We want to become more responsible. More responsible for what we consume and more responsible for ourselves. We want to change the narrative from the “thank you, next” mindset in the fashion industry. We want to show the world that the old can become new; that what yesterday was out of trend, today can be brought to life; that there is another way.

Velements promotes the concept of upcycled fashion. It’s not a new concept, yet it is a powerful one. The fast-fashion industry produces globally 1 billion clothing articles per year. To put it a different way, almost 3 million articles per day and yet 85% of our old clothes end up in a landfill.

It’s time to change that. Give your old clothes a chance. Give creativity a chance to reduce waste.

FOUNDER ADRIAN VELE

Mission

Our mission is to create a culture of fashion responsibility by promoting upcycled clothing. How does that work? We take old clothes and transform them into something new and unique. This way we aim to limit the excessive consumption of fashion items. We want to show the world that with a little imagination and ingenuity something old can become even more valuable than something new.

Objectives
- increase awareness regarding the effects of fast-fashion consumption, as well as the manufacturing process;
- encourage responsible consumption;
- encourage the idea of offering a second chance to old clothes.
